I am currently a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am pursuing a Master of Science in Quantitative Biology and Bioinformatics. I previously earned a Bachelor of Science in Statistics and Data Science from the University of California, Santa Barbara. 

As a member of Dr. Russell Schwartz’s lab at CMU, I develop automated data pipelines and machine learning classifiers to predict cancer tumor metastasis from blood draw samples collected at multiple time points.

I am passionate about expanding my expertise in machine learning and data-driven research. I am actively seeking a summer internship opportunity where I can apply, and further refine, my skills in statistics, mathematics, data engineering, and machine learning.
